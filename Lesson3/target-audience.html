Target Audience

The target audience is going to be for those who want a quick place to get a very quick weather update on what it's going to be doing that week and for an individual day.

Persona 1

Name: Kayla Johnson

Demographics

  • Mother of 2
  • Married
  • Surfer

Scenarios

  • Teddy wants to take his family to the beach on the weekend and needs to make sure the weather will be nice
  • Teddy wants to see if the weather will be warm enough for him and his family to go surfing
  • He needs to check to see what type of gear they need to bring, if they'll need to bring clothes for cold weather or if they can expect nice weather

Persona 2

  • Drew Andrews
  • Single
  • Skier

Scenarios

  • Drew wants to know if he needs to know the average temperature for his week of skiing so he can prep extra layers
  • He needs to see what type of snow conditions there will be, so he can bring either his powder or his park skis
  • He needs to know if he needs to bring sun screen in case of a sunny day to help prevent from sunburn from the refelction of the snow
